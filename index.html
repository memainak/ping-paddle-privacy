We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect via the App includes:

We do not explicitly collect personally identifiable information (like your name or email address) unless you voluntarily provide it to us through support channels or feedback.

Device Data

We may automatically collect device information, such as your mobile device ID, model, and manufacturer, operating system, version information, and system configuration information, device and application identifiers.

Usage Data

We collect data regarding your gameplay, including scores, preferences, and how you interact with the App. This data helps us improve the user experience and the mechanics of the game.

Sensors Data

The App uses your device's motion sensors (gyroscope and accelerometer) strictly for gameplay mechanics (controlling the paddle). This data is processed locally on your device in real-time and is neither stored nor transmitted to external servers.
